Common Questions

Frequently asked questions.

How do I add a new AI agent?
Go to your workspace dashboard → AI Agents → Add New Agent. Select the channel, give it a name, upload your training documents, and activate.
How long does workspace activation take?
Most workspaces are reviewed and activated within 1–2 business days after you complete the onboarding form and payment.
Can I upgrade my plan mid-month?
Yes. You can upgrade at any time. The difference is prorated and charged on your next billing date.
How does the affiliate commission work?
You earn 25% of every subscription payment made by clients you referred — recurring, for the full lifetime of their subscription. Payouts are monthly.
What file types can I upload to train my AI?
You can upload PDF, DOCX, Markdown (.md), and JSON files up to 40MB each. The more context you provide, the more accurate your agents become.
How do I connect my own WhatsApp or Instagram?
After your workspace is activated, go to Integrations in your dashboard and follow the step-by-step connection guide for each channel. It takes about 5 minutes.
